UltrusMods

Mods made by UltrusBot


Released Mods

Mob Origins

Mob Origins is an origins addon that currently adds over 10 unique origins, each with different powers and abilities. If you are looking to use some powers from this in a datapack, you can check out the wiki.

Missing Wilds

Missing Wilds strives to add all the content that was dropped or scrapped from the Wild Update. This includes things like fallen logs, and the birch forest changes from the concept art shown at Minecraft Live 2021.

Hexxit Gear (Fabric)

Hexxit Gear is a remake of the classic Hexxit Gear mod, which was featured in the Hexxit modpack.

Smelting Touch

Smelting Touch adds a single enchantment: Smelting Touch, which can be applied to tools. It causes item drops from blocks broken with a tool to be smelted.

Glow Banners

Allows you to make banners glow, by right clicking them with a glow ink sac.

Size Shifting Potions

Size Shifting Potions adds potions to the game which all affect your size in various fun ways.

Extra Sponges

Extra Sponges adds new types of sponges to help clear out larger areas of water. It also includes a lava sponge for lava clearing.

Loading Screen Tips

Adds useful tips to the loading screen.

Lucky Ducks

Adds cute lil rubber ducks.


Planned Mods

The Everfrost

The Everfrost is a dimension plagued by ice and snow. Many creatures corrupted by the cold wander this dimension. The Everfrost contains remnants of a story lost to time, told through structures littered across the world.
